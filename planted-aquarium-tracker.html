Why log your planted tank?

Algae outbreaks, melting stems and stunted growth almost never appear out of nowhere — they trail a parameter that drifted. When you record every water test, you can scroll back and see that your NO₃ crept from 20 to 40 ppm the week before the green spot algae started, or that pH swings always follow a missed water change. Patterns that are invisible day-to-day become obvious across a timeline.
